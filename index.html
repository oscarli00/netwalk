How to play Netwalk

Rotate the tiles so that every tile has a connection to the power source.
Note: the board is wrapping which means connections at the edge of the board are able to connect to the opposite edge.

  • Left click: rotate counterclockwise
  • Right click: rotate clockwise
  • Middle mouse button / Spacebar: lock rotation
